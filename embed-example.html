About This Project

Water-Opt is a decision support tool built for Central California rice growers facing the annual choice between growing rice or fallowing their land and selling conserved water rights. The tool integrates real-world data from:

  • Hydrology: NRCS SNOTEL stations and DWR Bulletin 120 forecasts
  • Markets: USDA ERS Rice Outlook price data
  • Land Use: CA DWR Statewide Crop Mapping
  • Compliance: California water transfer regulatory framework

Key Features

  • Profit Comparison: Real-time calculation of growing vs. fallowing profitability
  • Breakeven Analysis: Determine the water price threshold for decision-making
  • Sensitivity Analysis: Tornado charts showing impact of parameter variations
  • Hydrology Scenarios: Dry/Median/Wet water year planning
  • Spatial Visualization: 20,000+ rice fields mapped in Sacramento Valley
  • Regulatory Compliance: Built-in guardrails and water transfer requirements
